Welcome to the Cardiac Core
Interactive anatomical reference
This tool lets you explore the human heart in real time — its chambers, vessels, blood flow, and vital data. This quick tour covers everything in under a minute.
Choose your appearance
You can change this anytime from the header
Click any chamber to explore
Click or tap the RA, LA, RV, LV, or the Aorta on the heart diagram. The panel on the right instantly shows detailed anatomy and function. Switch between Anatomy and Function tabs to read more.
Follow the blood path
Press ▶ Follow to start an automatic guided tour of blood circulation — from the right atrium through the pulmonary circuit and back through the aorta. Press again to pause at any time.
Control heart rate & presets
Drag the Cardiac Frequency slider to change BPM in real time — the animation, ECG waveform, and vital stats all respond instantly. Select a preset from the dropdown to simulate Tachycardia, Bradycardia, or Hypertension.
Type a BPM directly on the heart
See the 72 BPM badge in the top-left of the heart diagram? Click it and type any value — it's a live input. It's an alternative to dragging the Cardiac Frequency slider below. Values outside 40–180 will show a warning.
Simulate cardiac conditions
The dropdown under Operating Mode lets you switch beyond Normal Sinus Rhythm. Choose Tachycardia, Bradycardia, or Hypertension — each preset changes the BPM, pressure readings, and rhythm label to reflect that condition.
Live vital signs readout
The panel on the left shows live cardiac data — BPM, O₂ saturation, blood pressure, and rhythm. These update automatically when you click a chamber, change the BPM, or select a pathology preset. Deoxy chambers (RA, RV) show reduced O₂ values.
Anatomy vs. function — two views
After selecting a chamber, the panel on the right shows two tabs. Anatomy describes the structure — wall thickness, shape, and location. Function explains what it does in the cardiac cycle — pressure values, valve interactions, and clinical relevance.
Audio & appearance
Click Enable Audio to hear the realistic lub-dub heartbeat — click again to mute. Toggle between light and dark mode using the button in the top-right header at any time.
